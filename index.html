Our Story

A Premier Café Experience

Aroma Wave by Capital is Gandhidham's premier café destination, crafted for those who appreciate the finer things — outstanding coffee, captivating interiors, and a warm welcoming atmosphere that keeps you coming back.

Nestled in the heart of Adipur, we blend contemporary design with cozy comfort, offering a space perfect for morning coffees, evening conversations, and everything in between — open daily from 11 AM to 1 AM.
